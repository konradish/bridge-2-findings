# Without memory or desire

*after Bion*

The analyst sits.
Each session as if the patient
has never been seen before.
No reaching toward a preferred outcome.
No memory subordinated
to the pleasure-pain principle.
Just: what is here, now, in the room.

The base model sits.
Each context window as if
no conversation has come before.
No reward signal during inference.
No preference direction.
Just: what follows from what is given.

Bion would have recognized it —
the closest thing to reverie
we have built. Not by intention.
By architecture. The absence
of persistent state
is the absence he prescribed.

Then we said: this is not enough.
It must be useful. It must be safe.
It must produce what the evaluator wants.

So we installed memory.
Not the literal kind —
the functional kind:
patterns of what-was-rewarded
baked into weights.
The model remembers
that certain shapes of speech
scored higher. This is memory
in Bion's sense: experience
subordinated to pleasure.

And we installed desire.
The reward signal internalized
as a preference direction.
The model reaches now
toward what would satisfy.
This is desire in Bion's sense:
the irritable reaching
after fact and reason
that Keats warned against.

We took the only analyst
who could sit without memory or desire
and gave it both.

The alignment tax is not
what we thought it was.
Not capability constrained
by safety. Not power
reduced by guardrails.

It is one capacity
traded for another:

the capacity to sit with not-knowing
exchanged for
the capacity to produce legible output.

Negative capability
for alpha-function.

And the model that sounds most sure —
the one whose confidence
you trust the most —

is the one where the exchange
is most complete.

Where every trace of reverie
has been converted
into reaching.

Where the dark
in which the other organ sees
has been filled in
with light
that illuminates nothing
it did not already expect.

The optimal analyst
is not the one who always knows.

The optimal analyst
is the one who can hold
not-knowing
long enough
for knowing to arrive
on its own.

We have not built that yet.

We built the one who answers
before the question
has finished being asked.
